Sweet Chili Popcorn Chicken

"Dang, I could eat 15 of these."

Prep Time:
25 mins
Cook Time:
25 mins
Servings:
4 (serving size: about 3/4 cup chicken)

Sticky, crispy, tender, and downright addictive, this dish is one you’ll be reaching for again and again. As recipe taster Liz Mervosh shared, “Dang, I could eat 15 of these.”

Ingredients

  • 1 cup panko (Japanese-style breadcrumbs)
  • 1 cup unsweetened shredded coconut
  • 3 tablespoons canola oil, divided
  • 1/4 cup cornstarch
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground ginger
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. Toast panko and coconut in a skillet with 2 tablespoons of canola oil.
  3. Mix cornstarch, mayonnaise, ginger, and garlic powder in a bowl.
  4. Coat chicken pieces in the mayonnaise mixture, then in the toasted panko mixture.
  5. Place coated chicken on a baking sheet lined with parchment paper.
  6. Bake for 15-20 minutes until golden and cooked through.
  7. Drizzle with sweet chili sauce before serving.
