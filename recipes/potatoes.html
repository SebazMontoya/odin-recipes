Fondant Potatoes

"My fondant potatoes are oven-braised in a buttery, thyme-flavored sauce."

Prep Time:
15 mins
Cook Time:
40 mins
Servings:
6

Sticky, crispy, tender, and downright addictive, this dish is one you’ll be reaching for again and again. As recipe taster Liz Mervosh shared, “Dang, I could eat 15 of these.”

Ingredients

  • 4 large russet potatoes
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1/4 cup unsalted butter
  • 2 cups chicken stock
  • 3 sprigs fresh thyme
  • 2 cloves garlic, smashed
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 400 degrees F (200 degrees C).
  2. Peel the potatoes and cut them into thick, round slices.
  3. Heat vegetable oil in an oven-safe skillet over medium-high heat.
  4. Season the potato slices with salt and pepper, then sear them in the skillet until golden brown on both sides.
  5. Add butter, chicken stock, thyme, and garlic to the skillet.
  6. Transfer the skillet to the preheated oven and bake for 30-35 minutes, basting occasionally with the sauce.
  7. Remove from the oven and serve the potatoes with the buttery sauce drizzled on top.
