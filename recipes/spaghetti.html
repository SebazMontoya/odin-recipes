Million Dollar Spaghetti

Million dollar spaghetti is the perfect combination of noodles, lean ground beef sauce, cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream. It's unbelievably good!

Prep Time:
10 mins
Cook Time:
1 hr
Servings:
8

“The cream cheese brings out a rich and creamy flavor while the pasta sauce gives it a real zing,” according to Lucky. “One thing I did differently is that I mixed my pasta noodles in with the cream cheese, cottage cheese, and sour cream. This is a delightful dish that can feed a small family or a large gathering! It's definitely my go-to meal!”

“I also like to add a sprinkle of fresh parsley or basil on top for a burst of freshness,” Lucky adds. “Sometimes, I even experiment with adding a pinch of red pepper flakes for a little heat. This recipe is so versatile and forgiving, you can easily adapt it to suit your taste preferences or dietary needs. It's always a hit at potlucks and family dinners!”

Ingredients

  • 1 pound spaghetti noodles
  • 1 pound lean ground beef
  • 1 jar (24 ounces) marinara sauce
  • 1 cup cottage cheese
  • 1 cup cream cheese, softened
  • 1/2 cup sour cream
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C).
  2. Cook the spaghetti noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef over medium heat until browned. Drain excess fat.
  4. Add marinara sauce to the skillet with the beef and simmer for 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine cottage cheese, cream cheese, and sour cream until smooth.
  6. In a greased baking dish, layer half of the cooked spaghetti noodles, followed by the cheese mixture, then the remaining noodles.
  7. Pour the beef and marinara sauce mixture over the top and spread evenly.
  8. Sprinkle mozzarella and Parmesan cheese over the top.
  9. Bake in the preheated oven for 25-30 minutes, or until the cheese is melted and bubbly.
  10. Remove from the oven and let it cool for 5 minutes before serving.
